Attack of the Clones

Homepage

Attack of the Clones is the second film in the Star Wars prequels. It follows Anakin Skywalker as he protects Padmé Amidala, who is being targeted by assassins. Meanwhile, a galaxy-wide conflict escalates as the Galactic Republic creates a clone army to fight the Separatists. Anakin's growing love for Padmé and his internal struggle foreshadow his eventual fall to the dark side.
